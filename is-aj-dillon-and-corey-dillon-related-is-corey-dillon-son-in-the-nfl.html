Is AJ Dillon and Corey Dillon related? Is Corey Dillon son in the NFL?

AJ Dillon is an American football running back who plies his trade in the National Football League for the Green Bay Packers. AJ Dillon is a three-time First-team All-ACC, a one-time ACC Rookie of the Year and a one-time ACC Offensive Rookie of the Year, all in 2017.

Corey Dillon is a former American football running back who played in the National Football League for the Cincinnati Bengals and the New England Patriots. Corey Dillon played college football for the Washington Huskies and was drafted by the Bengals in the 1997 NFL Draft.

Is AJ Dillon and Corey Dillon related?

AJ Dillon and Corey Dillon are not related in any way except for their surnames and their profession. Many fans are surprised about the fact that Corey and AJ are not family members despite the striking resemblances between them.

Is Corey Dillon son in the NFL?

Corey Dillon is blessed with three children but none of his sons plays in the National Football League.
