Donal Logue career earnings, salary and net worth

One of the wealthiest and most well-known TV actors is Donal Logue. The television series Grounded for Life, as well as his more recent roles in movies and on Law & Order: SVU, are what made him most famous.

Logue has played prominent roles in well-known movies like The Tao of Steve, Sons of Anarchy, Vikings, and the crime drama Terriers. He was the lone boy in a family of four born to Irish parents in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada on February 27, 1966.

The 56-year-old spent the majority of his youth in El Centro, California, and pursued a history degree at Harvard University following high school. His first film appearance, aside from a few TV movies, was in the 1992 movie Sneakers.

Along with his wife and two sons, Logue owns residences in both Los Angeles and Oregon.
