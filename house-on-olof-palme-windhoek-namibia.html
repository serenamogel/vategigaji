House On Olof Palme, Windhoek, Namibia

Located in the heart of Katutura, House On Olof Palme is a hotel with free parking, free wifi, facilities for disabled guests & 4.4 guest reviews rating . House On Olof Palme has one of the most exquisite menus in the city , the on site restaurant treats guests to really fine dining. Guests can also unwind at the bar and enjoy an exotic drink. House On Olof Palme has family friendly rooms that are well-spaced and are centrally air conditioned. High speed complimentary Wi-Fi access, flat screen TV, mini bar, tea & coffee making facilities.
