Elijah Moore contract, salary and net worth explored

He played college football at Ole Miss and is said to be one of the best wide receivers. He was born to his parents on March 27, 2000, in Sunrise, Florida, United States and is currently at the age of 22. In this article, we will dive into his career to know his contract with the Jets.
