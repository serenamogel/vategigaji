Ryan Terry Clark is a former American football safety who played in the National Football League.

He played college football at Louisiana State University and was signed by the New York Giants as an undrafted free agent in 2002. Clark also played for the Pittsburgh Steelers and the Washington Redskins.

Clark won a Super Bowl ring with the Steelers in Super Bowl XLIII and made a Pro Bowl in 2011.

He has a sickle cell trait condition which made it dangerous for him to play at high altitudes, such as in Denver, Colorado.
