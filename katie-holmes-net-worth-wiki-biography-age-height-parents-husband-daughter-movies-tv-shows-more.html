Katie Holmes Net Worth, Wiki, Biography, Age, Height, Parents, Husband, Daughter, Movies & TV Shows

Katie Holmes was born on 18th December 1978 in Toledo, Ohio, the U.S. She’ll turn 44 years old in 2022. Her father is Marin Joseph Holmes Sr. who is an attorney and a former college basketball player. Her mother is Kathleen who is a homemaker and a philanthropist. She has four siblings in all, including three sisters, and a brother.

Katie Holmes began her acting career in the year 1997, her first breakthrough role was that of Joey Potter in the television series “Dawson’s Creek” which ran from 1998 to 2003, followed by “The Kennedys” in 2011, “Ray Donovan” in 2015, and “The Kennedys: After Camelot” in 2017, among others.

Her first film was 1997 released “The Ice Storm”, after which, she starred in several successful films like “Go” in 1999, “Wonder Boys”, “The Gift” in 2000, and so on. Others are “Batman Begins” in 2005, “Mad Money” in 2008, “Jack and Jill” in 2011, etc. Her latest films are 2020 released “Brahms: The Boy II”, and “The Secret: Dare to Dream”. At the end of July 2022, came out “Alone Together”.

Katie Holmes got married to Hollywood actor Tom Cruise in November 2006 after dating for one and a half years since April 2005 and getting engaged in June 2005. Their daughter was born in April 2006 named Suri Cruise. Holmes filed for divorce in June 2012 which was finalized in July 2012.

Katie Holmes is currently dating musician Bobby Wooten III. They have been spotted together on multiple occasions and their relationship has reportedly been approved by her daughter Suri.
