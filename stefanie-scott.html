Biography

Stefanie Scott was born in Chicago, IL on December 6, 1996. Actress who became known for her role as Lexi Reed on Disney’s A.N.T. Farm, for which she won a Young Artist Award in 2011. She provided the voice for the character Moppet Girl in the 2012 film Wreck-It Ralph and appeared as Julianne in Frenemies. She starred as Lexi Reed with Jake Short in A.N.T. Farm, starting in 2011. As a child, she earned roles on stage, commercials, and print ad work, and made her film debut as Katie in the 2008 film Beethoven’s Big Break.
