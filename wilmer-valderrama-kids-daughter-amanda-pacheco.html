Wilmer Valderrama Kids: Meet His Daughter Nakano Oceana With His Partner

In January 2020, Wilmer Valderrama became engaged to Amanda Pacheco, eleven years his junior.

On December 21, the couple announced they were expecting their first child; the daughter, Nakano Oceana, was born on February 15, 2021.

People state that the name was inspired by a trip the pair took to Japan.

Valderrama and Pacheco are yet to get married. Despite getting engaged at the start of 2020, they remain unmarried two years later. 

They have not commented on the reason behind the gap, but it does not appear to be attributed to hiccups in their relationship. 

The Latinx power couple initially kept their relationship low-key as Valderrama had just ended a six-year-long relationship with Demi Lovato.

However, on New Year’s Day of 2020, they surprised the world when they revealed their engagement after a short and relatively secretive dating period.

“‘It’s just us now’ 01-01-2020,” Valderrama captioned a photo of him proposing to Pacheco at a La Jolla beach in California.

Pacheco also had some sweet things to say. Taking to her Instagram story, she wrote a message to her late mother.

According to Extra, she wrote, “Every time I talk about him, I can see your giant smile light up in my mind, and I can hear your mischievous laugh like you knew he was going to come into my life and change it forever.” 

She added that she had faith in everlasting love and marriage. She said she was terrified to start a family but could do it with “the most incredibly perfect life partner.”

Wilmer Valderrama Fiancée: Who Is Amanda Pacheco? 

Amanda Pacheco is an American model, divemaster, and social media star.

She was born in San Diego, California, to her parents, Tracy Morgan and Armando Pacheco.

The American star has also appeared in several films, including The Good Magician (2019), Sawman (2018), and Turbo Racer: Alpha (2017).

As a model, Pacheco works with different agencies, such as the SCOUT Modeling Agency. She also promotes brands like Beyond the Beach and Goorin Bros.

Wilmer Valderrama Relationship And Dating History

Wilmer Valderrama met singer-actress Mandy Moore around 1999 when both were teens. They dated when Moore was “16 or 17.”

Valderrama, who is four years Moore’s senior, claimed that Moore had lost her virginity to him. She denied the claim.

In 2004, the actor dated another actress-singer, Lindsay Lohan. This time, he was six years his girlfriend’s senior.

Valderrama later had an on-and-off relationship with another singer-actress, Demi Lovato, from 2010 to 2016. The two met when Valderrama was 29 and Lovato was 17.

However, they only began dating after she turned 18. Lovato later released the song 29 in 2022, widely interpreted as a condemnation of her former boyfriend now that the singer herself was 29.

Nevertheless, Lovato did not explicitly confirm the speculation, simply stating, “I feel like the song says it all.”

According to Who’s Dated Who, Valderrama has also been in relationships with Minka Kelly (2012 – 2016), Avril Lavigne (2009), Ashlee Simpson (2005), Ariana Richards (1999), and Christina Milian (1998).
