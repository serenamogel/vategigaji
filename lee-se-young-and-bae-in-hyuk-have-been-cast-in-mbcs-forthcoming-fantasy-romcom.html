Lee Se-young And Bae In-hyuk Have Been Cast In MBCs Forthcoming Fantasy Romcom

On Tuesday, March 21, South Korean media source Top News revealed that Red Sleeve actress Lee Se-young has been verified to appear as the protagonist in MBC’s forthcoming fairytale and romcom drama The Story of Park’s Marriage Contract alongside actor Bae In-hyuk. The latter was only recently revealed to feature in the February thriller.

The Story of Park’s Marriage Contract is based on the same-named webtoon and centers around a contract marriage in which a female lead from the Joseon Era discovers herself in modern-day South Korea in 2023 and goes into a contract marriage with the male lead. Because of its plot and main ensemble, many K-drama fans are hoping that the forthcoming magic and mystery drama will offer them something to look forward to in the future months.

The Story of Park’s Marriage Contract, which stars Lee Se-young, is based on a webtoon created by Angelique, Kim Neo UI, and Dotda.

Red Sleeve actor Lee Se-young will play Park Yeon-woo, a Josean Era lady whose spouse dies on the first day of their marriage. When someone threw her into a well, the former would not even have time to grieve his demise. Park Yeon-woo would soon find herself in 2023 South Korea, where everything was foreign and strange to her senses. She tries to find methods to travel back in time to her hometown, where she resided and lost her spouse on their wedding day. There, she encounters someone who resembles her one-day spouse Kang Tae-ha uncannily.

Several media sources have already verified that Baek In-hyuk will play Kang Tae-ha, a modern-day loner who does not believe in marriage or physical intercourse. Park Yeon-woo and Kang Tae-ha would resolve to marry on the basis of a compact in which they would not have any physical intercourse but would observe certain guidelines and live in the same house for their separate personal benefits.

However, things will take a dramatic change when Park Yeon-woo falls for Kang Tae-ha, who mimics her late spouse, and her plan to return to Joseon is jeopardized. Fans are looking forward to seeing how the pair will portray themselves in The Story of Park’s Contract Marriage. The forthcoming time travel and mystery drama starring Lee Se-young and Bae In-hyuk is based on Angelique and Kim Neo UI’s webtoon of the same name, which was drawn by Dotda.

Yoo Sun-ho will also co-star with Bae In-hyuk and Lee Se-young in the forthcoming drama The Story of Park’s Contract Marriage as Kang Tae-min. The production team has not yet divulged any additional information about the director, screenwriters, or other ensemble members.

In short, learn more about K-actors Lee Se-young and Bae In-hyuk.

Lee Se-young is a well-known and well-known South Korean actor who came to prominence after appearing in the landmark Red Sleeve opposite Lee Jun-ho. Fans admired Lee Se-young’s character in the K-drama for his audacious and adventurous personality. Since her debut in the industry in 2003, the actor has featured in a number of plays, including Jewel in the Palace, Memorist, Hit the Top, and Doctor John, among others.

Meanwhile, Bae In-hyuk is a young Korean performer who rose to worldwide prominence after starring as the protagonist in the SBS serial Cheer Up. Since his debut in 2019, the performer has featured in several plays, including Why Her? Under the Queen’s Umbrella, At a Distance, Spring is Green and others.
