New Lupe Fiasco Songs Off Of Lasers

Recommended Videos

Dropping next week is Lupe Fiasco’s highly anticipated album ‘Lasers‘. We’ve already heard four tracks from it, they include Words I Never Said, The Show Goes On, All Black Everything and Never Forget You. Today, two more were leaked. Beautiful Lasers and Break The Chain hit the internet today and no joke, I think they’re the best songs that we’ve heard from Lupe in a very long time.

Break the Chain features Eric Turner and Sway with producing credits going to iSHi. Beautiful Lasers features MDMA and producing credits go to The Future. With the addition of these two songs, we’ve now heard six tracks from ‘Lasers’ and I can safely say that this album won’t disappoint. Will it be worth the incredibly long wait? That remains to be seen, but one thing is for sure, fans won’t be let down with Lupe’s latest.

Check out the tracks below and let us know what you think.
