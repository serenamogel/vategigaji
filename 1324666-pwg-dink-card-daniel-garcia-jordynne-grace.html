Daniel Garcia, Jonathan Gresham, Jordynne Grace, And More Announced For PWG DINK

has announced the card for PWG Dink, its next event, which will be held at The Globe Theatre in Los Angeles, California, on November 6.

The show is named after Alan “Dink” Denkenson, a sports bettor who regularly attended PWG shows until he passed away earlier this year.

Among other bouts, Daniel Garcia will defend the PWG World Championship against Jonathan Gresham. Plus, IMPACT Knockouts World Champion Jordynne Grace will make her PWG debut; she will face Masha Slamovich in a rematch from IMPACT Bound For Glory.

Jonathan Gresham faces Daniel Garcia for the PWG World Title on November 6th at DINK!

— PWG (@OfficialPWG) October 14, 2022

The card is as follows:

  • Daniel Garcia (c) vs. Jonathan Gresham
  • Jordynne Grace vs. Masha Slamovich
  • “Speedball” Mike Bailey vs. Lio Rush
  • The Dark Order (Evil Uno & Stu Grayson) vs. The Rascalz (Trey Miguel & Myron Reed)
  • Davey Richards vs. Shane Haste
  • Titus Alexander vs. Rey Horus
  • Latigo, Laredo Kid, & Black Taurus vs. Aramis, Bandido & Komander
