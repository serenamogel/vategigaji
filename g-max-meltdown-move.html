G-Max Meltdown (Japanese: キョダイユウゲキ Kyodai Meltdown) is a damage-dealing Steel-type G-Max Move introduced in Generation VIII. It is the exclusive G-Max Move of Gigantamax Melmetal.

G-Max Meltdown inflicts damage and subjects the target and its allies to Torment, preventing them from using the same move twice in a row.

Its power and whether it is a special or physical move depends on the move it is based on.
