Jon Dickson

Jon Dickson (Japanese: トシヤ Toshiya) is a character of the day who appeared in Johto Photo Finish. He is a Pokémon Trainer from Sento Cherry Town.

Jon was a competitor in the Silver Conference. He made it all the way to the semifinals, where he battled Harrison. After defeating Harrison, he went on to win the finals. His Championship Trophy was given to him by Mr. Goodshow.

Rapidash
Jon Dickson's Rapidash appeared in Johto Photo Finish, where it battled a Pokémon Trainer to win the Silver Conference. With one Pokémon left between them, it was a battle between the Trainer's Rhydon and Jon's Rapidash that determined the victor. Rapidash attacked Rhydon with an extremely powerful attack, making Rhydon faint and winning its Trainer the Silver Conference.

None of Rapidash's moves are known.
