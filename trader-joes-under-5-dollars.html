Cheap Trader Joes Meals (and Snacks!) Under $5

The first thing any loyal Trader Joe’s shopper will tell you is how affordable it is to shop there. Followed by how yummy the frozen food tastes. Followed by how many great vegan products they have. Followed by…well, I think you get the idea. And they aren’t wrong! TJ’s grocery aisles are chock-full of products that deserve a spotlight for their affordability and deliciousness. We compiled a list of our favorite cheap Trader Joe’s meals under $5 for you and your budget-shopping needs—and for any future historians looking for quantitative data on our current economic crisis. Enjoy! 

Best Salad Kit

Lemony Arugula Basil Salad Kit – $3.99Trader Joe’s

I’m not sure the Sporked team has ever been more excited about a salad kit, to be honest. As our editor-in-chief Justine Sterling writes, “I have bought this Trader Joe’s salad kit again since first trying it in this taste test. Yes, using my own hard-earned cash. That’s as much of an endorsement as I can possibly give a product.” This zesty, peppery Lemony Arugula Basil Salad Kit includes carrots, crunchy almonds, shaved parmesan, and some secret magical ingredient that makes anyone who tries it fall immediately in love. True love, for only $3.99? Say less.

Best Classic Potato Chips

Trader Joe’s Ode to the Classic Potato Chip – $1.99

Many chip brands have tried, dared, dreamed of unseating TJ’s Ode to the Classic Potato Chip, our the best plain potato chip we tried in two separate taste tests. These potato chips just taste so earthy and fresh, which perfectly complements their saltiness and finger-licking greasiness. 

Credit: Liv Averett / Trader Joe’s

10/10

Best Artisan Bread

Olive Fougasse – $3.49Trader Joe’s

We’d be remiss not to mention the single best Trader Joe’s product we tried in 2023: the Olive Fougasse bread. My colleague Jordan Myrick called it “the best store bought bread” they’d ever had, comparing it to an oven-baked olive bread from a French bakery. This loaf has a chewy, soft inside, crispy exterior, and moist olives that infuse the loaf with flavor. This was a Sporked Award recipient, and the fact that you can find such high quality bread under $4 definitely makes this one of the best deals at Trader Joe’s to date.

Best Sandwich Bread

Sourdough Sandwich Bread – $3.49Trader Joe’s

You might be looking for bread that’s a little more functional than the olive loaf that looks so pretty, a part of you doesn’t want to even touch it. Trader Joe’s Sourdough Bread was our runner-up for the best TJ’s bread, right behind the Olive Fougasse, and earned the Sporked seal of approval from self-proclaimed Bay Area sourdough snob herself, Justine.

Best Frozen Indian Meal

Chicken Tikka Masala – $4.49Trader Joe’s

TJ’s has a wide range of frozen Indian food, more than most major grocery stores. They’ve really cornered the market on individual, microwavable Indian dishes, and we’re thankful for that, since these are some of the best cheap Trader Joe’s meals you can find. Their chicken tikka masala is nearly impossible to fault—it’s so creamy and fragrant, and the chicken cuts like butter.

Best Frozen Chicken

Mandarin Orange Chicken – $4.99Trader Joe’s

Winning our TJ’s vs. Panda Express Takeout taste test, the Mandarin Orange Chicken from TJ’s has some of the best quality chicken from the freezer aisle we’ve tried. It comes with multiple sauce packets (a nice touch, thank you, TJ’s), which makes it easy to mine a few dinners out of this—and all at a $4.99 price point. This might be the GOAT of cheap Trader Joe’s meals.

Best Frozen App

Parmesan Pastry Pups – $4.99Trader Joe’s

Of course, where would any of us be without TJ’s Parmesan Pastry Pups? The greasy, buttery, decadent parmesan taste of these are unmatched and still our #1 pick for frozen TJ’s appetizers. And pro tip: poke them with toothpicks to avoid wasting too many napkins on greasy fingers. These crowd-pleasers should be a staple at any party you’re throwing—just make sure you’ve got some vegan and vegetarian appetizer options, too.

Best Frozen Veggie App

Mini Vegetable Samosas – $3.99Trader Joe’s

Speaking of vegetarian appetizers, have you and these TJ’s veggie samosas cordially met yet? We’re just so happy these crispy, flaky, veggie-packed samosas exist. They might be a little bit smaller than the kind you can find at an Indian restaurant, but you also get a dozen of them for only $3.99. There’s not a ton of major grocery stores out there selling frozen samosas, so really, when it comes to cheap Trader Joe’s meals (or snacks), these are a steal.

Best Dip

Everything and the Elote Greek Style Yogurt Dip – $3.79Trader Joe’s

Mexican street corn in a dip? If you’ve ever passed this over because you were confused about what that would taste like, we beg you to reconsider. This is, hands down, our favorite dip at Trader Joe’s. It blends the freshly grilled smokiness of elote with a tart yogurt base. The corn kernels mixed in are a little sweet and burst in your mouth. For only $3.79, a dip of this caliber is one of the best deals at Trader Joe’s.

Best Cheese

Creamy Toscano Cheese Soaked in Syrah – $4.99

Artisan cheese for under $5? The Creamy Toscano Cheese Soaked in Syrah is our favorite TJ’s cheese to date, and absolutely one of the best deals at Trader Joe’s. Every element in this flavorful cheese, from the tart, wine-soaked  to the crunchy cheese crystals, creates something harmonious, elegant, and addictive. Not to be confused with their syrah-soaked Toscano cheese spread and dip, which also ranked quite high on our ranking of the best TJ’s cheese, this is a cheese block that’s delicious enough to slice up and eat on its own, no accouterments required.

Best Dairy-Free Cheese

Shredded Parmesan Cheese Alternative – $3.99Trader Joe’s

Those in search of a yummy dairy-free parmesan cheese understand that the struggle is, indeed, very real. TJ’s shredded parmesan earned 10/10 sporks in our vegan shredded cheese taste-test. It’s nutty, salty, and finishes creamy. It’s also at least a dollar cheaper than the other vegan cheeses we tried and loved, so that’s a plus.
