Can you delete or edit a review on Airbnb?

Yes, you can edit a review on Airbnb if you are the guest who left the review. You can edit the review for up to 48 hours after it’s submitted. However, once the review has been edited, the original version will not be accessible. On the other hand, hosts cannot edit guest reviews, and neither guests nor hosts can completely delete reviews on Airbnb.
