Shelly Garrett net worth: Shelly Garrett is an American actor who has a net worth of $10 million. Shelly Garrett is known as "The Godfather of Urban Theater". He started his theater career in 1986. His stage play Beauty Shop was seen by more than four million people and holds box office records in more than 50 cities including New York, Los Angeles, Atlanta, Dallas, Miami, Philadelphia, and Chicago.

Garrett starred in the 1979 movie Love at First Bite. He appeared in episodes of the television series Gimme a Break! from 1986 to 1987 and 227 in 1988. He also appeared in the TV documentary Backstage on the Black Stage in 2006. He has received keys to the city from more than 20 American cities. Garrett was awarded a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in 1992. Beauty Shop inspired the movies The Salon, Hair Show, and Beauty Shop, as well as the TV series New Attitudes.
