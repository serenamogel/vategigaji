Replace the battery with a new one

Remove the detector from its mounting plate using the provided tools. To remove your Vivint smoke detector from its mounting plate, find it and twist it counter-clockwise to release it from the mounting plate.

 To test the smoke detector, press and hold the test button for a few seconds. Also, do you know how to tell whether the batteries in your smoke alarm need to be replaced?

Battery life is dwindling. A smoke alarm will “chirp” about once per minute when the battery in the smoke alarm becomes weak, alerting you that the battery needs to be changed as soon as possible. Please keep in mind that only the gadget with a low battery will chirp. All of the other alarms that are linked should be silenced.
