Is Trace Cyrus Adopted? Who Is Trace Cyrus Father? Did Billy Ray Cyrus Adopt Brandi And Trace?

American musician, Trace Cyrus, was adopted when he was young by Billy Ray Cyrus, his mother’s second husband, and lived with them part of the time in Thompson’s Station, Tennessee.

Upon his adoption, his name was legally changed to Trace Dempsey Cyrus.

Who Is Trace Cyrus Father?

Trace Cyrus’ biological father is Baxter Neal Helson, an American drummer. He is the ex-husband of actress and producer Tish Cyrus.

At the time of his divorce from Tish, his daughter, Brandi, was only two years old, and his son, Trace, was only a few months.

After their divorce, he lost custody of his children, and Kish got custody of children, and she took away the kids from him.

Did Billy Ray Cyrus Adopt Brandi And Trace?
Tish and Billy Ray Cyrus married in 1993 after their daughter, singer Miley Cyrus, was born. The two then had Braison and Noah Cyrus together, and Billy Ray adopted Tish’s children, Brandi and Trace Cyrus.

Billy Ray Cyrus first got married to Cindy Smith, but after five years, they divorced.

After that, he had relationships with Kristin Luckey and Leticia “Tish” Finley, with whom he fathered children both born in 1992 – Christopher Cody and Destiny Hope.
