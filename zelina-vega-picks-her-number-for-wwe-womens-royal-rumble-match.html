Zelina Vega Picks Her Number For WWE Womens Royal Rumble Match

Zelina Vega revealed that she’ll be making her presence felt at the Women’s Royal Rumble match during a previous episode of SmackDown. Now, she finally knows her number.

Click here for our full coverage of WWE SmackDown.

WWE just dropped a new video on their Twitter. In the video, Zelina Vega can be seen picking her number for the match. The video ends with Zelina looking at her number and laughing without revealing her order of entry.

@ZelinaVegaWWE has picked her number for tomorrow’s #RoyalRumble! What does she have planned?!

Zelina Vega will join Rhea Ripley, Candice LeRae, Liv Morgan, Raquel Rodriguez, Shayna Baszler, Emma, Bayley, Dakota Kai, IYO SKY, Lacey Evans, Xia Li, and 22 other participants for the rumble.

The 2023 Royal Rumble premium live event will emanate from the Alamodome in San Antonio, Texas, tomorrow night. You can check out the complete card for the event below.

  • Men’s Royal Rumble Match
  • Women’s Royal Rumble Match
  • Roman Reigns (c) vs. Kevin Owens – Undisputed WWE Universal Championship Match
  • Bianca Belair (c) vs. Alexa Bliss – RAW Women’s Championship Match
  • LA Knight vs. Bray Wyatt – Pitch Black Match
